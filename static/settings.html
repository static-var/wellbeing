Careful zone

These actions are significant. Resetting your companion clears its memory and personality. Deleting your account removes everything permanently.

Reset your companion?

This will clear your companion's memory and personality settings. It's like starting fresh — your companion won't remember previous conversations.

Your account and preferences will be kept.

Delete your account?

This will permanently delete your account, all conversations, preferences, and any connected services. This cannot be undone.

All your data will be erased immediately.
