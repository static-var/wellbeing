A private companion for difficult days and ordinary ones.

You do not have to explain yourself here.

Wellbeing is a small, self-hostable companion for the moments when you want to talk, reflect, or simply be met gently. It remembers what you share. It checks in sometimes. It does not ask you to perform.

Read what it is, and what it is not
Honesty first

This is support, not authority.

Wellbeing is not therapy. It is not crisis care. It will not diagnose, prescribe, or act like a substitute for a clinician, lawyer, or emergency professional. If you are in danger or in immediate crisis, please contact local emergency services or a real crisis line right away.

What it actually does

Simple things, done carefully.

  1. It listens.

    You can say what is on your mind without turning it into a task list, a score, or a performance.

  2. It remembers.

    Recent sessions, preferences, boundaries, and recurring themes stay available so you do not have to start from zero every time.

  3. It checks in gently.

    You can choose quiet, optional check-ins at the times that fit your life. They are easy to ignore and easy to change.

  4. It stays close to you.

    Web and Telegram work today. The point is continuity: the same companion, the same memory, on the surface that feels easiest.

Why the privacy claim matters

Privacy is structural, not optional.

Self-hosted by design

Wellbeing runs as a lightweight Rust app with local runtime state in SQLite. Your conversations live on your machine or your server, not ours.

Your own Gemini key if you want it

Personal inference uses your Gemini API key directly. The app keeps setup simple and handles model choice for you instead of making you manage a picker.

Open source and inspectable

The prompts, guardrails, memory behavior, and routing are visible in code. If you want to understand it, you can. If you want to change it, you can.
