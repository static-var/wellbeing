What we believe

  • 🫧
    Emotional safety first Everything is designed to feel calm, slow, and safe. No urgency, no gamification.
  • 🌱
    Not a replacement for professional care Wellbeing is a supportive listener — a buddy, not a therapist. We encourage seeking professional help when needed.
  • 🕊️
    You're always in control Change your preferences, reset your companion, or delete all your data — no questions asked, no guilt.
  • ✨
    Presence over productivity This isn't about optimizing yourself. It's about noticing how you feel and having a space where that matters.
